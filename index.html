How To Order?

Follow these simple steps to bring home your handmade treasures.

1. Browse & Note Favourite

  • Explore our handmade collections.

  • Copy the Product ID of the piece by tapping on button.

2. Reach out to us

  • Tap the ‘Order on WhatsApp/Instagram’ button on the product page.

  • Send us a message to place your order.

3. Share Your Details

Send the following:

  • ✅ Your Full Name

  • ✅ Full Address (with PIN code)

  • ✅ Alternate Contact Number

  • ✅ Product ID(s)

4. Payment Info

  • We’ll confirm your order via message and share dispatch details shortly after.

  • Pay safely with Cash on Delivery.
    We deliver across India—only pay when it reaches your doorstep!
